Our Research Areas

We are focusing on making multi-modal intelligent systems with AI!

Vision and Imaging Research

Bio-inspired Camera-based Vision

High-speed and HDR vision problems using event cameras and bio-inspired approaches for next-generation visual sensing systems.

360 Camera-based Vision

Digital human, neural rendering, and omnidirectional image understanding for immersive visual experiences.

High-level Vision Problems

Semantic segmentation, 2D/3D object detection and recognition for intelligent scene understanding.

Low-level Vision Problems

HDR imaging, image/video restoration and enhancement techniques for improved visual quality.

3D Scene Understanding and SLAM

Robotics, XR, HCI, and simultaneous localization and mapping for spatial intelligence.

NeRF-based 3D Reconstruction

Domain adaptation, knowledge transfer, and neural radiance fields for 3D scene representation.

AI and Machine Learning

Our lab focuses on cutting-edge learning methods in computer vision, including Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), self-supervised and semi-supervised learning approaches, and transfer learning techniques. We develop novel algorithms that enable machines to learn more efficiently with less labeled data, bringing us closer to human-level visual intelligence.

Computer Vision for AR/Metaverse

We explore avatar generation, scene modeling and understanding for extended reality applications. Our research in multi-modal AIGC (AI Generated Content) for the Metaverse includes depth estimation, motion estimation and compensation in virtual environments, enabling more realistic and immersive digital experiences.
