About BioRAI Lab

Welcome to the Bio-inspired AI and Robotics (BioRAI) Lab at NTU. We are dedicated to advancing the frontiers of artificial intelligence and robotics through bio-inspired approaches. Our research focuses on making multi-modal intelligent systems that can perceive, understand, and interact with the world in more natural and efficient ways.

Our lab explores various cutting-edge topics including bio-inspired camera-based vision, 360° camera vision, 3D scene understanding, SLAM, high-level and low-level vision problems, and multi-modal learning methods. We aim to publish top-tier research in world-class conferences and journals while fostering the next generation of researchers in AI and robotics.

We are focusing on making multi-modal intelligent systems with AI!

Addison Lin Wang is an Assistant Professor in the School of EEE, NTU-Singapore. His research interests focus on bio-inspired artificial intelligence, computer vision, and robotics systems. He leads the BioRAI Lab in developing cutting-edge multi-modal intelligent systems.

Our Research
