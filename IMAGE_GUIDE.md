## Tips

1. **Image Sizes:**
   - Logo: Recommended 200-300px width, transparent background
   - Background: Recommended 1920x600px or larger for full-width header

2. **File Formats:**
   - Logo: PNG (for transparency)
   - Background: JPG (for better compression) or PNG

3. **Backup:**
   - Keep backups of original template images before replacing
